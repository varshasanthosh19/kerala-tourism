ABOUT ALAPPUZHA DISTRICT

Alleppey is a beautiful city located in Kerala, India. If you’re looking for a place to relax and soak up some culture, Alleppey is the perfect destination for you. There are so many wonderful tourist places in Alappuzha that will make your trip unforgettable. The best time to visit Alleppey would be during March and April when there are fewer tourists present in the city. This will allow you to enjoy your trip without having to compete for a taxi or bus seat with other travellers. Whether you plan to spend just one day in Alleppey or are planning a whole vacation around the area, we’ve put together this list of 21 Alappuzha tourist places you absolutely cannot miss when visiting the region.

Beauty Of Alappuzha District

Alappuzha (or Alleppey) is a city on the Laccadive Sea in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's best known for houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters, a network of tranquil canals and lagoons. Alappuzha Beach is the site of the 19th-century Alappuzha Lighthouse. The city's Mullakkal Temple features a traditional design. Punnamada Lake's snake boat races are a well-known annual event.
